About Us

Leading Plastic Waste Management Solutions

We are committed to creating sustainable solutions for plastic waste management. Our platform connects communities with recycling facilities, incentivizes proper waste disposal, and educates about circular economy principles. Since 2020, we've diverted over 500 tons of plastic from landfills through our innovative reward-based system.

Our Process

How It Works

Collection

Users deposit sorted plastic waste at our smart collection centers equipped with AI-powered sorting technology.

Get Coupon

Earn digital coupons based on quantity and type of plastic deposited. Track your earnings through our mobile app.

Avail Reward

Redeem coupons for discounts at partner stores, public transport credits, or donate to environmental causes.
